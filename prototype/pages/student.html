Chapter I · The Realization

The career advantage isn't built
in your final semester.
It's built across all four years.

Looking back from the Mature Professional stage, one thing is clear: my career journey didn't begin the day I graduated. It began on the first day I walked into university — I just didn't know it at the time.

Fortunately, I discovered this early. By the time I graduated, my CV wasn't limited to coursework or academic achievements. It already reflected meaningful internships, volunteer work, national and international competitions, student organization leadership, and experiences that demonstrated real-world capability — not just academic potential.

Those experiences made my CV stand out significantly from other graduates applying for the same positions. Looking back, I recognize that this was an advantage — and one that many students unfortunately never have the opportunity to build before graduation.
