Chapter I · The Realization

Your career doesn't start after graduation—
it starts the moment you begin preparing for it.

Looking back from the Mature Professional stage, one realization stands above the rest:

The career journey doesn't begin after you graduate. It begins on the first day you walk onto campus.

By the time I graduated, my CV wasn't just coursework and grades. It already carried internships, student organizations, volunteer work, national and international competitions, student exchange — the experiences that made it stand out from every other applicant's.

I was fortunate to realize it early. Unfortunately, not every student gets that kind of luck.

"
