## 2. The Five Engines

### 2.1 Compass — Discovery & Navigation
*"Where am I, and what's my highest-leverage next move?"*

- **Readiness Diagnostic** — 15-question assessment producing a personalized gap analysis (skills, materials, network, confidence, market knowledge). The free, generous front door.
- **Career Explorer** — role/industry discovery with honest data: real day-in-the-life content, salary bands, demand trends from Atlas, pathway difficulty from *your* starting point.
- **Pathway Engine (AI)** — generates a unique 90-day action plan; re-plans dynamically as the user progresses and as market demand shifts.
- **Meta, the AI Career Coach** — persistent conversational coach with memory of the user's journey; answers navigation questions, reframes setbacks, routes to the right engine ("your bottleneck this week is interview reps, not another course").

### 2.2 Forge — Preparation Suite
*"Practice like the ones who were coached."*

- **CV Studio** — builder + real ATS scoring against target job descriptions (keyword match, format compliance, impact quantification), with specific rewrite guidance.
- **Interview Coach** — AI mock interviews calibrated to role/level/weak areas; STAR-framework feedback; longitudinal pattern detection ("you consistently understate leadership impact").
- **Case & Presentation Practice** — structured case interview drills, presentation recording with delivery feedback.
- **Assessment Prep** — practice banks for aptitude/psychometric tests with adaptive difficulty.
- **Simulations** — task-based, employer-co-created job simulations (the Forage mechanic) producing shareable, verified completion credentials.

### 2.3 Circle — Community & Mentorship
*"The network you weren't born into."*

- **Peer Matching** — same-role, same-stage practice partners; sessions framed as *a gift to the other person*, not a transaction.
- **Cohorts** — time-boxed small groups moving through pathways together; accountability, warmth, shared wins.
- **Mentorship Marketplace** — verified professionals offering sessions (free community slots + paid premium); alumni auto-invited to mentor after placement.
- **Resilience Studio** — rejection-recovery programming, confidence practices, identity work; emotional support as a first-class product surface, not an afterthought.
- **Community Feed & Sub-communities** — industry/role/city groups, AMA events, win celebrations.

### 2.4 Bridge — Opportunity & Employers
*"Get into the room, not just ready for it."*

- **Talent Marketplace** — users opt in to be discoverable by employers on verified signals (simulation completions, readiness scores, skill credentials) — capability-first, pedigree-blind options.
- **Employer Portal** — simulation builder, branded challenges, candidate pipeline management, cohort sponsorship, hiring analytics (conversion, quality-of-hire, cost-per-hire).
- **Jobs & Referral Pathways** — partner listings plus alumni referral routing ("3 Metanoia alumni work here and mentor").
- **Application Command Center** — tracking, follow-up cadences, offer comparison and negotiation guidance.

### 2.5 Atlas — Workforce Intelligence
*"The insider's view of the market, for everyone."*

- **Market Intelligence Feed** — demand trends, skill velocity, salary benchmarks by role/region ("demand for your target role in Jabodetabek is up 23% this quarter").
- **Readiness Prediction** — honest outcome probability modeling ("users with your profile typically receive interviews after ~2.3 more peer sessions and 1 CV iteration").
- **Intelligence Library** — the insider knowledge corpus: how ATS actually works, hidden job market mechanics, negotiation norms.
- **Institutional Insights (later)** — aggregated, anonymized workforce intelligence for universities, employers, and governments — the long-term data business and the "infrastructure" positioning.

## 3. The Passport (Identity Spine)

Every engine reads from and writes to the **Passport** — a lifelong, portable, user-owned career identity:

- verified skill credentials & simulation completions,
- readiness score history (growth is visible, not just state),
- contribution reputation (mentoring, peer sessions, community help),
- career history and trajectory data,
- privacy-controlled: the user chooses what employers can see; discovery is opt-in.

Long-term: exportable, standards-aligned (Open Badges / verifiable credentials), the "career passport" that outlives any single employer or platform session.

## 4. The Flywheel

```
 More users → richer peer pool & community (Circle)
     → better preparation quality (Forge)
        → better placement outcomes (Bridge)
           → alumni return as mentors (Circle ↺)
           → employers see hiring ROI → more employers, more simulations (Bridge ↺)
              → more outcome + market data (Atlas)
                 → smarter pathways & coaching (Compass ↺)
                    → better results per user → word of mouth → more users ↺
```

Three reinforcing loops:
1. **Regeneration loop (social):** placed users become mentors — supply of guidance scales with success.
2. **Employer loop (commercial):** hiring wins fund content co-creation and pipeline demand.
3. **Data loop (intelligence):** every outcome trains better prediction and personalization — the compounding moat.

## 5. Reputation & Progression System (Gamification Done Honestly)

Principle: **progress must map to real-world capability, never to engagement theater.** No streak-shaming, no confetti inflation.

| System | Mechanic | Why it's honest |
|---|---|---|
| **Readiness Score** (0–100, per target role) | Composite of CV quality, interview practice performance, simulation completions, market alignment; always explainable | Mirrors actual hiring readiness; moves when capability moves |
| **Journey Chapters** | Orientation → Preparation → Connection → Transition → Alumni | Progress = life progress, not points |
| **Skill Credentials** | Earned via verified simulations & assessed practice | Employer-legible, portable to Passport |
| **Contribution Reputation** | Earned by mentoring, peer sessions, community answers; visible on profile | Makes generosity the highest-status behavior; powers the regeneration loop |
| **Momentum (gentle)** | Weekly intention → weekly reflection loop; no punitive streaks | Sustains habit without anxiety mechanics |
| **Milestone Moments** | First mock passed, first interview landed, offer accepted — celebrated in cohort | Community-witnessed wins, the emotional payoff |

## 6. Stakeholder Value Exchange

| Stakeholder | Gives | Gets |
|---|---|---|
| **Student/seeker** | Effort, practice, honest data, (later) premium fees | The full access infrastructure; verified signals; community; outcomes |
| **Alumni** | Mentoring time | Status, reputation, leadership development, giving-back identity |
| **Mentors (external)** | Expertise, sessions | Income (paid tier), reputation, talent visibility |
| **Employers** | Simulations, jobs, subscription revenue | Verified-skill pipeline, wider + better funnel, brand equity with early talent |
| **Universities** | Cohorts, licensing fees | Outcome dashboards, employability lift, curriculum integration |
| **Governments/institutions (later)** | Contracts, distribution | Workforce intelligence, mobility outcomes at population scale |
