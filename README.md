# Metanoia

**“Metanoia” (μετάνοια): a transformative change of heart and mind.**

A next-generation human capital platform — a **career operating system** — focused on democratizing access to career opportunities, institutional knowledge, mentorship, and workforce transformation infrastructure.

> The real problem is often not a skills gap. It is an **access gap**. Metanoia rebuilds the invisible infrastructure of career success — knowledge, coaching, networks, and employer access — as software, personalized by AI and open to everyone.

## The Five Engines

1. **Compass** — AI career discovery, readiness diagnostic, personalized 90-day pathways
2. **Forge** — CV Studio (real ATS scoring), Interview Coach, simulations, assessment prep
3. **Circle** — peer matching, cohorts, mentorship marketplace, resilience programming
4. **Bridge** — employer portal, opt-in talent discovery, jobs & referral pathways
5. **Atlas** — workforce intelligence: market signals, readiness prediction, insider knowledge

All engines read from and write to the **Passport** — a lifelong, portable, user-owned career identity.
